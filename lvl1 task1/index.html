Also Care The Beutiful Nature!!

All living and non-living things, such as plants, animals, rocks, water, and air, are considered to be a part of nature. By supplying necessary resources like food, water, and oxygen, it is critical in maintaining life on Earth. Also, it has several advantages for human health and wellbeing, including lowering stress levels and enhancing mood. But human activities like deforestation, pollution, and climate change are seriously stressing the environment and endangering the stability of ecosystems. For all living things, including people, to survive, nature must be preserved and protected.
